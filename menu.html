Connect Four with AI

About the Game

The game aims to develop a classic Connect Four game with an implementation of an AI opponent. The game will allow users to play against computer-programmed AI which will be performed at a difficult level. The AI will employ Minimax algorithm and Alpha-beta prunning to enhance its gameplay, providing a challenging experience for players to win.

Objective

  • Choose the ideal vertical section to place the token
  • Connect four in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
